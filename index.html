4 Pottery

studio and ceramics

4 Pottery is an upcoming pottery studio focused on hand building, creative play, and learning the fundamentals. Whether you’re brand new or already deep in your clay era, we’re building a space that’s welcoming, easygoing, and fun.

Targeting Toronto near Highway 401—between Allen Road and Leslie Street. Final address coming soon.

Before we open, we’re collecting a waitlist and getting feedback so the studio matches what the community actually wants. Add your name and let us know what matters most to you.
